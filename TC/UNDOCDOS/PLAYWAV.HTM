Playing WAV files in DOS

PLAYWAV will play a WAV file in DOS. It is a small and simple routine, but is distributed for the benefit of other programmers to use and learn (if applicable)
Version: 1.02b
Author: Ben Lunt (Forever Young Software(R))
Date: 19 Sept 1998

Assembler: MASM 5.10
Compiler: Quick Basic 4.5
Compiler: Turbo Pascal 6.x

Download the PlayWav zip file from here (31k) and unzip it (PKUNZIP 2.04g recommended) in a directory of your choice, then view PLAYWAV.TXT
(To download, I suggest right clicking the link and saving to disk)
