Pascal


This Pascal column is one of four columns that will be in every issue, with the other three being the other 'major' compilers/assemblers; Assembly, Basic, and C.

As with the other columns to there respected names, this column will be devoted to Pascal and Pascal programming. Each issue will have a new article or a continuing article about the Pascal language.

Unless otherwise noted, I will use the Turbo Pascal 6.x compiler for each of the Pascal listings in this column.

As this is the first issue, I have written an article in the Assembler Column on page 9 for the four languages. Starting with the next issue, this column will have its own article specifically about Pascal and the Pascal language. ¥
