The ascii character table contains 256 (0-255) characters.

The first 33 (0 through 32) are special characters used for different things
 such as tab, space, enter, esc, pagebreak, etc.

 0 = null
 7 = beep (bell)
 8 = backspace
 9 = tab
10 = LF
12 = Page Break
13 = CR
27 = ESC

Ascii characters 33 through 47 are the 'puncutation' chars.  Most sentence
 puncutaion and the normal math symbols (+ - / *).

Ascii characters 48 through 57 are the numberals '0' through '9'.

Ascii characters 58 through 64 are more of the 'puncutation' chars.

Ascii characters 65 through 90 are the capt. letters 'A' though 'Z'.

Ascii characters 91 through 96 are more of the 'puncutation' chars.

Ascii characters 97 through 122 are the small letters 'a' though 'z'.

Ascii characters 123 through 255 are the rest of the chars and are all
 different types of symbols which inlcude the box drawing symbols.
  *see below*
