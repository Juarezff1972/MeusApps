Getting todays weekday name

We will use service 2Ah of Interrupt 21h.

mov  ah,2Ah
int  21h

al = weekday (0-6) (0 = Sunday, 1 = Mon..., etc.)
dh = month (1-12)
dl = day (1-28,29,30, or 31)
cx = year (1980 through 2099)
